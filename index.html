Although you might not see them as you go about your day, there are birds everywhere, you may well have heard them.
Whether you are walking in the countryside, looking out of the window, sitting in the park, waiting for a bus or gardening. You may have been aware of the flitter of wings, the rustle of leaves, tiny chirps, or loud cawing, and wondered what was that!
This website is designed to be an easy and quick reference guide for the most common birds you may see here in the UK.
